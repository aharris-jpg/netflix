The new season drew just 2.4 million views in week one, trailing XO Kitty, The Lincoln Lawyer and Virgin River, despite a solid 62% audience score.

Netflix · True Crime Miniseries · 50% Cancellation Probability

The Witness hit No. 1 globally in week two with 10.1 million views.

By week three, that number collapsed to 2.8 million, a 72% drop, the steepest fall on this list.

50% Cancel Probability 10.1M to 2.8M in 1 week

Netflix · Drama · 47% Cancellation Probability

What the Numbers Mean for Canadian Viewers

Eighteen cancellations in six months isn't a slow year for Netflix. It's the new normal. Viewership in month one now matters more than reviews, franchise loyalty, or awards.

For Canadian subscribers watching the same queue as the US, the shows worth getting attached to are the ones posting strong week one numbers, like Man on Fire or Nemesis, not just the ones getting good reviews.

Streaming is getting faster and less forgiving every season. Patience for a slow-building hit is disappearing, and not just at Netflix.

The next cancellation announcement is never far away.

Methodology

What we measured. Every Netflix original series with a confirmed renewal, a confirmed cancellation, or an undecided status, as of June 30, 2026. 53 shows met that bar.

How confirmed shows were scored. An official renewal scored 0% cancellation probability. An official cancellation scored 100%.

How undecided shows were scored. Only shows with real viewership or critic data made the list. We weighted four factors: viewership trend (40%), critic and audience reception (20%), time since premiere without a decision, measured against Netflix's typical 28-day evaluation window (20%), and franchise or source material strength (20%).

What we left out. Lower-profile shows with no public viewership or critic data were excluded rather than guessed at.

One flagged conflict. Finding Her Edge has two trackers reporting opposite outcomes. We kept it in and flagged the disagreement instead of picking a side.

When the data was pulled. June 30, 2026. Netflix renewal news moves fast, so figures may shift after this date.

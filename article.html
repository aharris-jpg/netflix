Methodology

What we tracked. 52 Netflix originals as of June 30, 2026.

Confirmed shows. Official renewal = 0%. Official cancellation = 100%.

Undecided shows. Scored on four data points: Days Unresolved (35%), Weeks in Global Top 10 (25%), averaged RT critic, RT audience, and IMDb rating (25%), IMDb live popularity trend (15%). All figures verified directly from IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes on June 30, 2026.

What this is not. A comparative ranking. Not a guaranteed forecast.
